## What is the RSQKit?

The framework for research software excellence in EVERSE integrates community curation, quality assessment and best practices to foster high-quality, reusable research software across disciplines.

This collective expertise is captured in the [**Research Software Quality toolkit** (RSQKit)](https://everse.software/RSQKit/) — a living knowledge base designed to support software excellence throughout the research lifecycle.


## Who can use it?

By embedding the RSQKit and related services into the EOSC Science Clusters, EVERSE will demonstrate improvements in the quality of research software, promote standardisation and reuse, and supports recognition and career development for everyone who contributes to software — from researchers who code to Research Software Engineers (RSEs).
