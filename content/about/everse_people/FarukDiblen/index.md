Faruk Diblen is a [Senior Research Software Engineer at the Netherlands eScience Center](https://www.esciencecenter.nl/team/faruk-diblen/).

His work covers a wide range of topics, including FAIR software, open science, software sustainability, research software quality, visualization, and product development. He is a strong advocate for best practices in research software development and is active in the research software community.

Within the EVERSE project, Faruk is involved in [Work Package 2](/workpackages/02_best_practices/) and [Work Package 3](/workpackages/03_tools_and_services/). He is specifically contributing to the design and development of DashVERSE, a dashboard for visualizing research software quality assessment results and indicators.
